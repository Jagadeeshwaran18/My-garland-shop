Serving with Trust Since 1963

For over six decades, BBJM Garland has been more than just a flower shop.

Established in 1963, our garland shop has been serving customers with devotion, tradition, and trust for generations. We specialize in crafting fresh flower garlands using handpicked blooms, prepared daily to ensure natural fragrance and lasting beauty.

With decades of experience, we have proudly supported weddings, temple rituals, festivals, and special occasions. Our work reflects a perfect blend of traditional craftsmanship and modern reliability.

Our Specialties

Traditional wedding garlands, divine temple offerings, and decorative floral designs. Known for neat finishing and long-lasting freshness.

Why Choose Us

Unmatched experience, quality craftsmanship, and reliable service. We focus on your satisfaction above all else.

Our Process

From sourcing the midnight-fresh blooms to the final knot, every step is handled with expert care to preserve the life of the flowers.

Commitment

Timely delivery and fair pricing. We take pride in the trust our customers have placed in us for over 60 years.

Our Mission

"To preserve the art of traditional garland making while bringing the beauty of fresh flowers into every home and celebration."
